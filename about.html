About Coppell

A vibrant community in the heart of the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex

Our Story

Present

Today, Coppell is a thriving community of over 42,000 residents in the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex. We pride ourselves on excellent schools, beautiful parks, and a strong commitment to community resources. Our hub connects residents with the services, programs, and opportunities that make Coppell a wonderful place to live, work, and raise a family.

From Past to Future

Originally settled in the 1840s and renamed Coppell in 1892, our city transformed dramatically after DFW Airport opened in 1974. From a small farming village to a vibrant suburban community, we've maintained our values while growing stronger. Looking ahead, we continue to expand our community resources and services, ensuring every resident has access to the programs and facilities that support their wellbeing and quality of life.
